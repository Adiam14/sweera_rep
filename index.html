About Us

Rooted in Tradition, Designed for the Future.

"Sweera by Pine Trunk is more than a brand - it's a movement to preserve and promote the art of handmade products. Our skilled artisans blend tradition with contemporary design to bring unique and sustainable tableware and baskets to your home. Each product tells a story of heritage and innovation, crafted with care to bring beauty and functionality into your home."

Vision

"Our vision is to blend the timeless art of weaving with modern design, creating sustainable, handcrafted products that inspire mindful living and connect communities. We strive to promote eco-friendly practices and empower welfare-driven initiatives for a conscious future."

Mission

"At Sweera by Pine Trunk, we empower artisans by preserving traditional craftsmanship, promoting sustainability, and offering eco-friendly products. Our mission is to bridge culture, creativity, and conscious consumption while creating opportunities and inspiring mindful living."

Our Story

Empowering Women through Artistry

"At Sweera by Pine Trunk, we believe in empowering women and preserving the rich heritage of traditional craftsmanship. Our collaboration with Tapasvi NGO plays a vital role in supporting rural artisans, especially women, in their journey towards financial independence. Every piece of handcrafted art tells a story of resilience, passion, and dedication, and we are proud to bring these beautiful creations to the world."
